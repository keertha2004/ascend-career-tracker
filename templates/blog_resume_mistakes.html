Top 10 Resume Mistakes to Avoid

  1. Using a generic resume for all jobs
  2. Missing keywords from the job description
  3. Too long or too short
  4. Poor formatting or fonts
  5. Typos or grammatical errors
  6. Lack of measurable achievements
  7. Not using action verbs
  8. Irrelevant information like hobbies
  9. No clear objective or summary
  10. Outdated information (e.g. old tech, high school)
